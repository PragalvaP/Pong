Destiny: First Memorable Moment

The first memorable moment in my favorite game Destiny is when the player picks up their first gun: The Khvostov. The gun itself was considered one of the worst in the game because it was just the starting weapon. However, it is something that many players (including myself) never got rid of. For me, the gun marked the beginning of an adventure that would last many years. While it was memorable at the time, it became something even more meaningful the more I played the game. Throughout the years, I would keep The Khvostov in my vault and use it every once in a while to remind myself of where I started. While the gun heavily crippled my character because of how weak it was, it was always something that held great nostalgic value.

The gun ended up making a comeback in the form of the highest rarity: Exotic. The first gun players picked up had so much meaning that the creators of the game decided to add an exotic version of it. The new exotic Khvostov could be used at the same level as the other high-level guns, but it never felt the same as the original Khvostov. While the new one could be used in high-level play, it could never replace the very first gun I picked up.
